40 gun models (pistols, rifles, shotguns, sniper rifles, submachine guns)
plus an Accessories subfolder (magazines, silencers, etc.) - 55 .fbx files
total, flattened into models/. Only FBX format was available for this pack
(no glTF, unlike the character packs) - Godot 4.7's built-in FBX importer
handles these directly, no conversion needed. No textures (flat vertex
colors), matching the men/women character packs' style.
