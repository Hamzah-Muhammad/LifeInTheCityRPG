10 characters (Adventurer, Casual, Formal, Medieval, Punk, SciFi, Soldier,
Suit, Witch, Worker), 24 shared animations, modular (each character split
into 4 swappable parts). Only the Individual Characters/glTF format was
downloaded (self-contained per-character glTF, no external textures) -
same convention as assets/quaternius_modular_men/.
