11 characters, 24 shared animations, modular (each character split into
4 swappable parts). Provided in FBX, OBJ, glTF, and Blender (.blend) formats.
This project keeps Humans_Master.fbx (game-ready, all characters+animations)
and Humans_Master.blend (editable Blender source).
